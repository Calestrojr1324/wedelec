Favorite Band

Linkin Park

Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in Agoura Hills, California, in 1996. The band's current lineup consists of vocalist/rhythm guitarist/keyboardist Mike Shinoda, lead guitarist Brad Delson, DJ/turntablist Joe Hahn, bassist Dave Farrell, co-lead vocalist Emily Armstrong, and drummer Colin Brittain.

Cueshe

Cueshe is a popular OPM rock band from Cebu City, Philippines. The band's name is a combination of the words "cue" and "shé". "Cue" refers to the Filipino snack of banana coated in caramel, while "shé" is a pronoun that refers to the band's former female vocalist. The band's current members are Ruben Caballero, Jovan Mabini, Fritz Labrado, and Cesar. Jay Justiniani was a member from 2001 to 2015.

Paramore
